STOCK UP

Mission

Connect people to available resources in upcoming disaster situations.

Product: Low-Fidelity Working Prototpye

Blast Surveys

The goal of these intial surveys was to gauge information regarding our problem space facing Disasters. Data collected became vital in narrowing our brainstormed ideas and defining our project's scope.

In-Person Surveys

In-person surveys conducted with various demographics of individuals geared our solution to their personal needs, expectations, and accessibility concerns.

Prototyping

Started with simple paper prototypes, user-tested and iterated upon to eventual designing of the user interface and compiling user-flows. These wireframes done in Figma(collaboration based barebones, cloud illustrator) and eventually moved to InVision for a Low-Fidelity HotSpot prototype. These were presented on mobile devices and features were added and improved based off user feedback.

Interviews

After confirming basic functionality and proper user flow, full interviews were conducted with people from each target field. We discussed past experiences in disaster situations, our product coupled with the prototype, and future implications of our solution to uncover the next steps.

Pop-Ups and Alerts
